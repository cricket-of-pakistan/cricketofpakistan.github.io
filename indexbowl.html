Paksitna Bowling Tallent

Pakistani bowler Saeed Ajmal bowled the crucial last over in an over-clapped World Twenty20 match at Eden Gardens, claiming two wickets to help secure the tournament win over New Zealand in February.

The bowler brought back memories of Pakistan’s first bowler to take a hat-trick at the international level, Abdul Qadir, who achieved the feat on November 10, 1984 in an innings and 74-run victory against Australia in Brisbane. Ajmal also becomes the first Pakistani bowler to take a hat-trick in an ODI game. He is the first bowler to take a hat-trick in both international and international matches. Pakistan’s bowler’s phenomenal bowling at international level was regarded as one of the outstanding achievements in international cricket. Ajmal’s ‘boxing action’ was hailed in the cricket world.
