The Pakistan Super League (PSL) is a professional twenty 20 cricket league in Pakistan. The league was founded in 2015, and it is organized by the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB). The league features six teams representing major cities in Pakistan, and it is considered on of the premier T20 cricket league in the world,

The Teams are Islamabad United, Karachi King, Lahore Qalandars, Multan Sultans, Peshawar Zalmi and Quetta Gladiators. The teams play against each other in a double round-robin format, with the top four teams qualifying for the playoffs. The league is typically held in February and March each year, with the final match being played at the end of the tournament. The league attracts top international cricketing talent form around the world and its also a platform for young cricketers in Pakistan to showcase their skills.
