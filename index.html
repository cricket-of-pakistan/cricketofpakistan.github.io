About Cricket

In world cricket, Pakistan is represented by its national cricket team, which holds Test and One Day International (ODI) status as a full member of the International Cricket Council (ICC). [1] Pakistan made their debut in international cricket in 1952 when they took on India in a four-day Test match at Delhi's Feroz Shah Kotla Ground. India prevailed by an innings and 70 runs. [2] [3] In the same series, Pakistan won their first Test match at the University Ground in Lucknow by an innings and 43 runs. [4] [5] In the 666 Test matches that Pakistan has played as of September 2022, they have won 145, lost 136, and had 164 matches finish in draws.
