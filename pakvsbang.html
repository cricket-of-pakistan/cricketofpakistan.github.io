Pakistan vs Bangladah

PAK vs Ban



Pakistan beat Bangladesh by five wicket in a gripping T20 world cup match to reach the semi-finals. Pakistan had been struggling in the earlier stages of the match, but with a strong batting performance, they managed to take the lead and held on to it to win. The final score of the match was 137-5 in Pakistan’s favour. Pakistan will now face India in the semi-finals.

Pakistan booked their spot in the next T20 World Cup with a dominant 5-wicket victory over Bangladesh in the final of the ICC World Twenty20 Qualifier. Pakistan had cruised to the final, defeating Zimbabwe and Netherlands by comfortable margins, but Bangladesh posed a challenge in the semi-finals. Bangladesh were restricted to 158-8 in their 20 overs, but Mohammad Irfan and Shoaib Malik combined to take four wickets in the last over, ending Bangladesh’s chance of qualifying. Pakistan will now look to defend their title at the 2022 edition in Sri Lanka.

Pakistan has been dominating T20 cricket recently, with their most recent win against Bangladesh coming by 5 wicket. The t20 world cup is scheduled to be hosted by Pakistan in 2022, and with their recent dominance, it is highly likely that they will be able to win the tournament.
